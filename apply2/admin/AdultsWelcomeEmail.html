On behalf of Osterley Cricket Club I would like to welcome you to Osterley Cricket Club and provide you with some information about the cricket activities we offer. Osterley Cricket Club provides opportunities for Adults of all age groups over 18 and young people between the ages of 5 and 18 to receive coaching and competition in the Middlesex Cricket Leagues. We participate in the following competitions:

  • Middlesex Championship League ­ Division 1 (1st XI)
  • Middlesex County Cricket League ­ Division 2 (2nd XI)
  • Middlesex 1987 League ­ Division 1 (3rd XI) and Division 2 (4th XI)
  • Middlesex County Cup
  • Middlesex County League T20
  • ECB National Cup
  • Middlesex Development League (U21s)

Cup Competitions run parallel to the regular league season. We also provide coaching through ex­Frist Class Cricketers who are also ECB Level 2 Qualified Coaches. All coaches have been screened for their suitability for working with our members and children.

Osterley Cricket Club has achieved its Club Mark Status on 2nd November 2009 (No:01474). ECB Clubmark is the ECB’s club accreditation scheme for clubs with junior sections. ECB Clubmark sets out required standards across four areas:

  • Duty of Care and Safeguarding Children
  • The Cricket Programme we offer
  • Knowing your club and its Community ­ One Game
  • Club Management.

Achieving ECB Clubmark accreditation is an acknowledgement that we take these issues seriously and that we are striving to provide a high quality and welcoming environment for young participants in cricket. We welcome parents and carers or guardians and all adult members to all training and competitions and value your support.Â 

Below is some information about training times and dates, and details regarding travel arrangements, kits and membership.

Training Sessions take place throughout the season and during the winter months. Your team captain or club representative will be able to inform you of the timings and dates for when these are scheduled. You will be notified through email/phone/social media or the Coach/Captain as to when you should attend your sessions.

Winter nets are due to end by the first week of April every year after which we move back to the Club and outdoor training, which you will be informed about.

Arrangements should be made for your travel to and from training sessions and league or cup matches. We would appreciate it if you can arrive promptly and are collected promptly at the end of every session, if you are not making your own way home.

Club Training Kits consist of a complete tracksuit with the Osterley CC Emblem along with a set of Whites and Sweater for Matches. Whites have to be worn for all league games as well as cup games, as required by the Laws of the game and the relative competitions. The cost for each Kit per adult is included in your annual subscription fees and can be collected from the Clubhouse before the start of the season.

Annual Membership Fee for adults is £150 per person, which has to be paid for every season you play for Osterley CC. Every season starts and ends in October of every year. If you participate in Open Age/Adult Cricket there are match fees that are covered in the Pricing Policy on the club website which is www.osterleycc.com.

We are grateful to you for submitting your membership and making the yearly subs payment. For safety reasons it is important that the club is informed of any medical condition or allergies that may be relevant should you fall ill or be involved in an accident at the club.

If you would like to talk to someone at the club about this information or your involvement with the club, please contact me on 07774573622 or e-mail me on omer@osterleycc.com.

Thank you for choosing Osterley Cricket Club for your sports activities and we will make every possible effort to ensure you progress and succeed at what you learn from us in a secure environment that we stride to provide.
